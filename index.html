TEACHING

My pedagogical view is to develop the curiosity among the students about the subject I am teaching such that the students should be able to have the conceptual understanding of the fundamental principles and ability to apply the concept in solving a real-world problem.

I strive to transfer the knowledge and my experiences to the students that I have gained during many years from interaction with industry, knowledge gathered through multiple research projects, theories learnt from vast amount of literature, experience in abstracting and modelling complex problems into simpler models.
